Context

This course is one to introduce the Adobe Creative Suite, specifically Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects. Having previous experience in these areas, I wanted to use this project to challenge myself.

Problem

This task was to create a personal branding poster to represent a client. This client was a classmate, one chosen by the students.

Approach

The approach started off with an interview with the client to understand who they are and what represents them. What followed as a mood board with central themes to the client. From there, specific elements were selected and assembled in the final poster.

Mock-Posters

Based on data collect in the interview and images sourced from Pinterest, multiple mock-up posters were created to test out design ideas. From these tests, the brick wall and legs stuck.

Final Design

The final design involved a brick background and a photograph of the clients legs. This poster was meant to capture the clients uniqueness and fascination with design. The shapes and legs showcase the abstract qualities wanted in this poster.

GIF Design

I took the the poster one step further and turned it into a gif. Flashing colours of the bricks and letters continue the uniquness
